GDPR and Data Retention Policy for Chameleon AI

Purpose

● The purpose of this policy is to outline Chameleon AI's approach to managing and retaining personal data in compliance
with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) regarding data retention practices.

Scope

● This policy applies to all departments and individuals responsible for data retention at Chameleon AI and covers all
types of data. It also applies to all geographic areas where Chameleon AI operates.

Data Retention Principles

● 1. Lawful Basis for Retention: Chameleon AI will only retain personal data if there is a lawful basis for doing so.
● 2. Data Minimization: Chameleon AI will only retain the minimum amount of personal data necessary to achieve the
purpose for which it was collected.
● 3. Data Accuracy: Chameleon AI will take reasonable steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and up-to-date.

Retention Periods

● 1. Chameleon AI will retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was
collected, including legal requirements and business needs.
● 2. Chameleon AI will review and update its retention periods on a regular basis to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Data Disposal

● 1. Chameleon AI will securely dispose of personal data once the retention period expires, through data deletion,
anonymization, or archival processes.
● 2. Chameleon AI will ensure that all personal data is disposed of in a manner that is compliant with applicable laws
and regulations.

Data Subject Rights

● 1. Chameleon AI recognizes the rights of data subjects under GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, and erase
their personal data.
● 2. Chameleon AI will respond to data subject requests in a timely and mannerly fashion within the context of data
retention.

Data Security Measures

● 1. Chameleon AI will implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect retained data
from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
● 2. Chameleon AI will monitor and review its data security measures regularly and update them as necessary to ensure
ongoing compliance and effectiveness.

Compliance and Governance

● 1. Chameleon AI will establish mechanisms for monitoring and ensuring compliance with this policy.
● 2. Chameleon AI will assign responsibilities for data retention and disposal, including the appointment of a Data
Protection Officer (DPO) where required.
● 3. Chameleon AI will conduct regular audits or reviews of its data retention practices to ensure compliance with
applicable laws and regulations.
● This GDPR and Data Retention Policy serves as a guiding document for Chameleon AI to establish consistent and
compliant data retention practices while safeguarding the privacy rights of individuals.
